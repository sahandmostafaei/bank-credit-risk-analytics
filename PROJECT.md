# Project Summary

## Title

Bank Credit Risk Analytics

## Motivation

Banks evaluate thousands of loan applications every day. Credit risk analytics helps financial institutions estimate the likelihood that a borrower will default, supporting more informed lending decisions and effective portfolio risk management.

## Objectives

- Explore consumer credit data
- Identify risk indicators
- Perform statistical analysis
- Develop a baseline predictive model
- Demonstrate analytical methods relevant to banking and finance

## Analytical Methods

- Exploratory Data Analysis
- Descriptive Statistics
- Correlation Analysis
- Logistic Regression

## Potential Applications

- Retail Banking
- Consumer Lending
- Credit Risk Management
- Banking Analytics
- Financial Risk Assessment

## Future Development

- ROC Curve
- Feature Importance
- Probability of Default (PD)
- Interactive Dashboard
- Portfolio Risk Analysis
