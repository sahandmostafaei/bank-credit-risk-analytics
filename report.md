# Credit Risk Analysis Report

## Executive Summary

This report presents an exploratory analysis of a credit risk dataset. The objective is to identify borrower characteristics associated with loan default and demonstrate techniques commonly used in banking analytics.

## Dataset

The analysis considers borrower demographic and financial variables including income, loan amount, credit history, and repayment status.

## Methodology

- Data cleaning
- Exploratory Data Analysis
- Summary statistics
- Risk indicator analysis
- Basic predictive modelling

## Key Findings

- Higher debt burdens are generally associated with greater default risk.
- Credit history is an important predictor of repayment behaviour.
- Income level influences loan performance.

## Conclusion

This project demonstrates a simple analytical workflow relevant to retail banking and consumer credit risk assessment.
